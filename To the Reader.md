To the Reader,
This book is not a translation.
It is not a historical reconstruction.
It is not an attempt to replace any faith tradition.
It is a narrative weaving together themes, myths, symbols, and teachings found throughout the Nag Hammadi texts and related Gnostic writings.
Some portions remain close to the ancient sources. Others bridge gaps between fragmented texts so the story may be read as a continuous journey.
Whether you read it as mythology, theology, philosophy, psychology, or poetry is entirely your choice.
My hope is not that you agree with it.
My hope is that it makes you think.
— Nubia
