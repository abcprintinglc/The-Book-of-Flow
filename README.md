# The Book of Flow

A mythic synthesis and storytelling project inspired by the Nag Hammadi library, Gnostic cosmology, spiritual remembrance, accountability, and return.

This is not a translation of the Nag Hammadi texts. It is a narrative weaving, written as a contemplative book that can later be revised, printed, hand-bound, or adapted into study-journal format.

## Working Structure

- `manuscript/` — chapter drafts
- `notes/` — source notes, revision notes, and production ideas
- `exports/` — future DOCX/PDF/print-ready exports

## Project Note

The eventual physical form may become a handmade contemplative book: half-letter pages, stitched signatures, blank reflection pages, stamped or letterpress-inspired elements, handmade paper, and covers made without leather or plastic.
